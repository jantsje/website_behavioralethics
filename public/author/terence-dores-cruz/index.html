Terence Dores Cruz

Postdoctoral researcher

University of Amsterdam

Terence is a postdoctoral researcher at CREED, the Center for Research in Experimental Economics and political Decision making of the University of Amsterdam. He is currently working within the Trustpath project. Terence uses interactive experiments, vignettes, surveys, experience sampling to answer questions about decision-making in the domain of cooperation, morality, and ethics.

Terence formerly worked within the FORCE-OF-GOSSIP project at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. For more information, see Terence’s personal website

Interests

  • Cooperation
  • Reputation and Information
  • Social Norms and Norm Enforcement
  • Social Psychology
  • Behavioral Economics
